Biography
Prem Rai

Prem Rai is a graduate student in Computer Information Prem Rai is a graduate student in Computer Information System from Westfield State University, MA. He works for Traf-Sys, as a Tech Support. He is currently enrolled on bootcamp session with MIT, toward becoming a Full Stack Software Developer. Prem attended Springfield Technical Community College and Westfield State University in MA, where he earned basic knowledge about programming which motivate him to have profession on it. He is proficient on several programming languages like JavaScript, Python, C#, Perl, Java, and Cobol. Back to his country in Nepal, during his teenage Prem used to help as a computer hardware assembler at his brother’s electronic repair center. These some little basic experiences and knowledge about computer drives him to explore more and earned degree on computer related field. In his spare time, he likes to play his favorite Fender Strat guitar. Currently, he joined a Local cover band ‘Aastra’ as a guitarist. Beyond that he loves photography, traveling, sports (Cricket, Volleyball, Soccer), and listening music of all genres. He is also an active volunteer member of his community where he serves as a president of Arts and Sports Department.
